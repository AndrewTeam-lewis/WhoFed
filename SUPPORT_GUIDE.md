# WhoFed Support Guide & Overview

Welcome to WhoFed! WhoFed is an app designed to help you organize and coordinate your pet's daily care needs across your entire household. Stop asking "Who fed the dog?" and always know exactly when your pets were last cared for, fed, or given their medication.

## How it Works

### Households
Your pet care revolves around a **Household**. A Household is a shared space where you and your family members, roommates, or pet sitters can manage your pets' routines together.

*   Every user can create a household and invite others to join.
*   The creator of the household is the **Owner**. The owner has full control over adding/removing pets, inviting/removing members, and changing member permissions.
*   Invited users become **Members**. Owners can configure whether members are allowed to log tasks, edit pet settings, or just view the schedule.
*   **Free users** can own up to **1** household, but can join as a member to an unlimited number of households. **Premium users** can create an unlimited number of households.

### Pets and Care Schedules
Within your household, you can add your pets and configure their unique schedules:
*   **Adding Pets:** Owners can add pets to the household. **Free users** can add up to **2 pets** per household, while **Premium users** can add an **unlimited** number of pets.
*   **Pet Avatars:** Free users can choose from a library of cute stock icons for their pets. **Premium users** can upload custom photos to feature their pet's adorable face!
*   **Scheduling:** You can create specific, recurring schedules for **Feeding**, **Medication**, or general **Care** (like walking, grooming, or litter box duty). 

### Daily Dashboard and Activity Logging
The core of the app is your daily dashboard. Here, you'll see all your pets' scheduled tasks for the day.
*   **Logging Tasks:** When you feed your pet or give them their medicine, simply tap the task on your dashboard to mark it as completed. Everyone in your household will instantly see the update. You don't have to wonder if the dog was fed—just check the app!
*   **Activity History:** Every completed task is recorded in the activity log. This gives you a clear, timestamped history of exactly who did what and when. **Free users** have access to limited recent history, while **Premium users** get **unlimited** access to their complete historical records - perfect for bringing to vet visits!

## Understanding Permissions

To keep your pet's schedule safe, WhoFed uses a simple permission role system.

**Owners** (the person who created the household) can always:
*   Add and delete pets.
*   Log tasks and edit pet settings.
*   Invite members and remove members.
*   Change the permissions of any member.
*   Delete the household entirely.

**Members** (people invited to the household) can always:
*   View the pet's schedule.

**Members** can *also* optionally do the following, if the Owner gives them permission:
*   **Log Tasks:** Tap items on the dashboard to mark them complete.
*   **Edit Pet Settings:** Change schedules, edit task names, or update pet details.

Owners can easily toggle these permissions on or off for each individual member from the household settings screen. This makes it easy to set up a trusted pet sitter who can *log* tasks, but perhaps cannot *edit* the underlying schedule.
